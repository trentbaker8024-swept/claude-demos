🎄 Q4 Holiday Planning
Q4 (Oct-Dec) typically sees 2x revenue compared to other months. Plan for this by building inventory now, increasing advertising budget in September, and preparing themed products for Thanksgiving, Black Friday, and Christmas.

Inventory Management Tips

• Reorder when inventory falls below 10 units for bestsellers
• Build 2 months of inventory ahead for Q4 (start in August)
• Track lead times for suppliers (typically 5-7 days)
• Maintain safety stock for top 3 products (3 weeks of supply)
• Use historical data to forecast seasonal demand
